# 🎮 Petra's Game — Witches & Princesses

> **Designed by Petra (age 6) ✨**
> A spooky-magical adventure game where a brave princess must escape the witch's labyrinth and find her way home!

## 🕹️ Play the Game

**[▶ Play Now on GitHub Pages](https://pedrofuentes.github.io/PetraGame/)**

Or open `index.html` in any modern web browser. No installation needed!

## 📖 The Story

*The princess has been lost in the Witch's dark world...*

She must navigate the **scary labyrinth** 🕷️, avoid the witch's spider web traps, pass through the **Princess Castle** 👑 where she'll be trapped by a spell, and finally escape to the **Real World** 🏠 — her home with its cozy cottage, garden of flowers, and warm sunshine.

But the witch wants to keep the castle magic for herself and will do everything to stop the princess from getting home!

## 🎮 How to Play

This is a **2-player game** played on the same keyboard!

### 👑 Player 1 — The Brave Princess
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| ↑ ↓ ← → | Move through the maze |
| SHIFT | ✨ Magic Blast — destroys nearby webs! (3 uses) |

### 🧙 Player 2 — The Wicked Witch
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| W A S D | Move through the labyrinth |
| E | 🕸️ Place a spider web (slows the princess) |
| Q | ☠ Place a SUPER WEB (bright green — traps for 4 seconds!) |

### ⏱️ Rules
- The princess has **120 seconds** to reach the Real World
- Regular webs **slow** the princess for 1.5 seconds
- Super webs **trap** the princess for 4 seconds — use magic blast to break free!
- When the princess reaches the castle, she's frozen but the magic **summons the witch**
- Once free, race to the Real World to win!
